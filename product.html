How is the Cleanbag

©

made?

1: Making the Bio-Plastic

Firstly we made the bio-plastics in our labs.
The plastic was created by our best bio-plastic
researchers. They created the perfect material
for our Cleanbag©. The bio-plastic is very
strong but also very elastic.

2: Designing the product

After creating the the bio-plastic we could
actually start designing the Cleanbag©. With
our skilled team of designers we quickly
realised our vision, a simplistic but extremely
useful bio-plastic bag was born.

3: Creating the product

But the hardest part was yet to come.
Actually creating the cleanbag© might've
been the hardest part of the project.
However, our highly skilled team of
engineers didn't see this as a problem.
And not much later the Cleanbag©
was done.
